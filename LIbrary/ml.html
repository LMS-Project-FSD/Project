MACHINE LEARNING


Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI where algorithms learn from data to make predictions or decisions. In today's tech era, ML offers automation, data insights, personalization, scalability, and a competitive edge. Career opportunities include roles such as Data Scientist, ML Engineer, AI Research Scientist, Data Analyst, and Business Intelligence Developer, spanning various industries. Key tools for ML include Python, R, TensorFlow, and scikit-learn, with professionals analyzing data, building predictive models, and deploying ML systems into production. The demand for ML expertise is increasing across industries, offering diverse career paths and opportunities for innovation. ML careers involve problem-solving and making a meaningful impact in domains like healthcare, finance, retail, and transportation.
