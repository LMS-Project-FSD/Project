CYBER SECURITY

Cybersecurity refers to the practice and techniques used to protect computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks, unauthorized access, or damage. It encompasses a range of methods to safeguard digital information and assets against cyber threats like malware, ransomware, phishing, and hacking. This field combines technology, processes, and controls to ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data. As our world becomes increasingly digital and connected, cybersecurity plays a crucial role in protecting personal, corporate, and national information assets from the constantly evolving landscape of cyber threats. Career opportunities in cybersecurity are vast, including roles like penetration tester, security analyst, incident responder, security software developer, cryptographer, and forensic expert. With the rise of cyber threats globally, there's a growing demand for cybersecurity professionals adept at mitigating risks and fortifying digital infrastructure. It involves not just securing information technology systems but also educating users about safe practices and staying ahead of potential vulnerabilities.
