HERE IS A ROAD MAP FOR AN ABSOLUTE BEGINEER TO LEARN CYBERSECURITY

Absolute Beginners (10 Weeks)

Week 1-2: Introduction to Cybersecurity Basics

Objective: Understand the fundamentals of cybersecurity and its importance.

  • Read introductory materials on cybersecurity.
  • Watch online videos explaining basic cybersecurity concepts.

Week 3-4: Learning about Assets and Threats

Objective: Identify different types of assets and common threats in cybersecurity.

  • Study materials on asset identification and threat analysis.
  • Complete quizzes and exercises to reinforce learning.

Week 5-6: Exploring Common Threats

Objective: Dive deeper into common cybersecurity threats like malware, phishing, and social engineering.

  • Research case studies of real-world cyber attacks.
  • Practice identifying and responding to simulated phishing emails.

Week 7-8: Understanding Detection and Response

Objective: Learn about detection mechanisms and incident response strategies.

  • Study materials on cybersecurity detection tools and techniques.
  • Set up a basic intrusion detection system (IDS) on a virtual machine and analyze alerts.

Week 9-10: Practical Application and Skill Development

Objective: Apply learned concepts in practical scenarios and develop hands-on skills.

  • Participate in cybersecurity capture-the-flag (CTF) challenges to practice skills in a gamified environment.
  • Work on a personal cybersecurity project (e.g., setting up a home firewall, securing personal devices) and document the process.
