ROAD MAP FOR INTERMEDIATE AI LEARNERS

Intermediate Learners (10 Weeks)

Week 1: Advanced Machine Learning Techniques

Objective: Explore advanced machine learning algorithms and techniques.

  • Study ensemble learning methods such as Random Forest and Gradient Boosting.
  • Implement ensemble models and compare their performance.

Week 2: Deep Dive into Neural Networks

Objective: Gain a deeper understanding of neural networks.

  • Study recurrent neural networks (RNNs) for sequential data analysis.
  • Explore long short-term memory (LSTM) networks and their applications.

Week 3: Advanced Topics in Deep Learning

Objective: Dive deeper into advanced topics in deep learning.

  • Study advanced activation functions and regularization techniques.
  • Explore advanced optimization algorithms such as Adam and RMSprop.

Week 4: Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)

Objective: Understand the concept and applications of generative adversarial networks (GANs).

  • Study the architecture and training process of GANs.
  • Implement GAN models for image generation tasks.

Week 5: Reinforcement Learning

Objective: Learn about reinforcement learning and its applications.

  • Study reinforcement learning algorithms such as Q-learning and deep Q-networks (DQN).
  • Implement reinforcement learning algorithms for simple environments.

Week 6: Advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Objective: Explore advanced topics in natural language processing.

  • Study advanced NLP techniques such as word embeddings and transformers.
  • Implement advanced NLP models for tasks like text classification and language generation.

Week 7: Computer Vision Applications

Objective: Explore advanced applications of computer vision.

  • Study object detection and image segmentation techniques.
  • Implement computer vision models for object detection tasks using frameworks like TensorFlow Object Detection API.

Week 8: Advanced Topics in AI Ethics and Bias

Objective: Understand the ethical considerations and biases in AI.

  • Study ethical guidelines and regulations related to AI.
  • Explore techniques for detecting and mitigating biases in AI models.

Week 9: AI Applications in Industry

Objective: Learn about real-world applications of AI in various industries.

  • Study case studies and examples of AI implementations in industries such as healthcare, finance, and manufacturing.
  • Explore the challenges and opportunities of deploying AI solutions in different sectors.

Week 10: Capstone Project

Objective: Apply the knowledge and skills acquired throughout the course to a comprehensive AI project.

  • Choose a project related to advanced machine learning, deep learning, NLP, or computer vision.
  • Define project goals, scope, and requirements.
  • Implement the project using appropriate AI techniques and libraries.
